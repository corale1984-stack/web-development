About Cats

Cats are fascinating, highly specialized predators with unique traits, including the ability to run up to 30 mph, 100+ vocalizations, and sleeping up to 16 hours a day. They have 30 teeth as adults, 18 toes, and use whiskers to navigate tight spaces. Their purr can repair muscles and bones, while their ears rotate 180 degrees using 32 muscles.

Jellyfish facts
Jellyfish are ancient, gelatinous marine creatures that have existed for over 500 million years, predating dinosaurs. they lack brains, hearts, and bones, utilizing stinging tentacles to paralyze prey.

Bunny sea slug facts
Sea bunnies (Jorunna parva)are tiny, toxic nudibranchs (sea slugs) found in the Indo-Pacific, famous for their appearance resembling furry rabbits. Typically under an inch long, their "ears" are actually sensory organs (rhinophores) used to smell, while their "tail" is a plume of external gills.

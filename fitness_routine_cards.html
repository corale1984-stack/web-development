My Fitness Routine Cards

This page shows a simple weekly fitness routine. Each card has its own content area, padding, border, and margin.

Morning Walk

Start the day with a stretch, and start with a nice walk.

Time: 20 minutes

Swim Workout

Do 200 swim, freestyle, or breastroke to improve catch and speed.

Time: 1hr 30 minutes

Strength Training

Practise pull-ups, push-ups, and planks to build body strength.

Time: 30 minutes

Cooldown Time

Try simple stretches to loosen muscles, release lactic acid, and relax your muscles.

Time: 10 minutes

Evening Walk

Go for a relaxing walk to stay active and refresh your mind.

Time: how long you want

Rest and Hydrate

Drink enough water and take proper rest after completing your workout.

Time: All day

Fitness Tip: A good routine includes exercise, rest, hydration, healthy food, and discipline and consistency.
